Deleting Documents


- deleteOne()
- deleteMany()

 find and delete with object "_id"
-deleteOne()
    - Used to delete a single document from the db.
    SYNTAX:
            db.collection.deleteOne({<filter>,<option>})
    
    ex:
        db.podcasts.deleteOne({_id:ObjectId("ftyguahsijodkl")})
    
-deleteMany()
    - Used to delete many documents, by categories, where every document has the same value&info.
SYNTAX: db.collection.deleteMany({<filter>, <option>})

    ex:
        db.podcasts.deleteMany({category:"crime"})
